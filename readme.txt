Allows you to insert your TripIt travel badge into your WordPress site

== Description ==

Once your TripIt Blog Badge has been activated on the TripIt site this widget will allow you to post your blog badge to your WordPress powered site.

To see a working example please visit http://samgabell.com

== Installation ==

1. Upload `tripit-badge.php` to your WordPress `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or install through the auto-installer
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Turn on your Blog Badge within your TripIt 'Settings' -> 'Publish Your Data' menu
1. Set the Widget Title and TripIt Badge ID within the widget 'Edit' panel found in your WordPress 'Appearance' -> 'Widgets' menu

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I find my TripIt Blog Badge ID? =

Your can find your Badge ID in the 'Settings' -> 'Publish Your Data' menu of your TripIt home page. Once the Blog Badge has been activated TripIt will provide you with a code snippet to insert the badge into your site. It will look something like the following.

`<div id="tripit-badge"><script type="text/javascript" src="http://www.tripit.com/account/badge/id/15C7F8E054C1388177DFDE35A8BD0720/div_id/tripit-badge/badge.js"></script></div>`

<br/>

All you need to do is copy and paste the id tag portion ('15C7F8E054C1388177DFDE35A8BD0720' in this example) into the space provided within the widget settings panel.

= I got my TripIt Blog Badge working but now it has disappeared? =

Check http://www.tripit.com/ if TripIt is performing site maintainence then your TripIt Blog Badge will cease to work until the site comes back online.
